Pulse AI Coach

Chat with your AI fitness coach anytime. Log meals and exercises by just telling Pulse what you ate or did. Get instant feedback, swap suggestions, and weekly performance insights.

Smart Meal Plans

AI-generated daily meal plans personalized to your macros, diet type, and taste preferences. Plans get smarter every day as Pulse learns what you actually eat.

🏋

Intelligent Workouts

100+ science-backed programs from beginner to advanced. Full Body, PPL, Arnold Split, Strong Curves — or let the AI build a custom plan from an 8-question wizard.

Food Recognition Engine

8-tier nutrition lookup chain spanning Open Food Facts, Edamam, FatSecret, USDA, and AI estimation. Get accurate macros for virtually any food on Earth.

Wearable Integration

Connect Apple Health, Google Health Connect, Fitbit, Garmin, or Oura Ring. Sync steps, heart rate, HRV, sleep stages, SpO2, and more — all reflected in your progress dashboard.

📊

Deep Progress Analytics

Volume trends, muscle balance radar charts, PR history, workout heat maps, sleep scores, weight tracking, body part distribution — see every dimension of your fitness journey.

💪

Active Workout Mode

Built-in rest timer with vibration alerts, superset linking, live set logging, exercise swap suggestions, coaching GIFs, warm-up & cooldown routines, and crash recovery.

🏆

PR Detection & Share Cards

Automatic personal record detection with celebrations during your workout. Share branded post-workout cards with your stats and a fun volume comparison illustration.

🛡

Injury-Aware Training

Tag your injuries during onboarding and EnerZee auto-swaps risky exercises for safe alternatives across 8 body zones. Training hard shouldn't mean training hurt.
