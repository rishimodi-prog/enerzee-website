Architecture

Four layers. Every recommendation passes through all of them.

This is what most "AI fitness apps" don't have.

01

Rules first.

Before any AI runs, deterministic rules check the basics — deficit, last session volume, sleep, safety flags. Rules are auditable, predictable, and never hallucinate.

02

Then an LLM reviews.

A language model reads the rule output as a prior, not a command. It can agree, suppress, modify, or ask one diagnostic question — but only with reasoning it can show you.

03

A self-critic checks the LLM.

If confidence is low, a second pass critiques the first. It looks for safety blind spots, unsupported claims, missing context. It can override the first model.

04

Outcome learning.

What worked for you last week feeds into next week's prior. Not as a black box — as a memory you can see, edit, and delete.

FAQ

Honest answers to the questions we get most.

How is this different from Cal AI / MyFitnessPal / MacroFactor?
Cal AI is a photo-logger; we're a reasoning coach. MyFitnessPal is a database; we're a thinking partner. MacroFactor adapts macros well but doesn't explain why or remember your context the way we do. EnerZee is built around the question those apps don't answer: why should you do this, and what worked for you last time?
Is the science-backed claim real, or marketing?
Real. We run a measurable Coach Reasoning Scorecard (9.5 / 10 today) with a public methodology. Every health claim in the app is grounded in research we can show you. If we ever can't cite it, we don't say it.
What about my data and privacy?
Memory is opt-in by category, fully inspectable, editable, and hard-deletable. Sensitive attributes are never inferred — only stored if you state them. Account deletion erases all of it. We do not sell data. We do not run ads.
Does it work for vegetarian, vegan, Indian, Mediterranean, or keto diets?
Yes. The food trust system pulls from USDA, IFCT (Indian Food Composition Tables), and OpenFoodFacts. Indian, South Asian, Middle Eastern, Mediterranean, and Western diets are all supported. Niche regional cuisines: file requests in-app and we add them.
