Frequently Asked Questions

What is EnerZee? ▼

EnerZee is an AI-powered fitness and nutrition app. It combines a personal AI coach (Pulse), smart meal plans, intelligent workout programming, food logging, wearable integration, and detailed progress analytics — all in one app.

Is EnerZee free to use? ▼

Yes! The Free tier includes full food and exercise tracking, food search across all databases, weight/sleep/water logging, active workout mode, PR detection, and 5 AI messages per day. The Pro subscription unlocks unlimited AI coaching, AI-generated meal and workout plans, and full progress analytics.

Which platforms is EnerZee available on? ▼

EnerZee is launching on both iOS and Android. Join the waitlist at enerzee.app to be notified when the app is available for download.

What wearable devices are supported? ▼

EnerZee integrates with Apple Health, Google Health Connect, Fitbit, Garmin, and Oura Ring. We sync steps, heart rate, HRV, sleep stages, SpO2, breathing rate, skin temperature, and weight data.

How does the AI coach work? ▼

Pulse is your personal AI fitness coach. Just tell it what you ate or did in natural language — "Had grilled chicken and rice for lunch" — and it logs everything automatically. You can also ask Pulse to swap meals, modify workouts, answer nutrition questions, and review your weekly progress.

How accurate is the nutrition data? ▼

EnerZee uses an 8-tier nutrition lookup chain spanning verified local databases, Open Food Facts, Edamam, FatSecret, USDA FoodData Central, and AI estimation. Each lookup is cross-validated with physics checks. While we strive for accuracy, all nutritional values should be treated as informed estimates.

Can I cancel my subscription? ▼

Yes, you can cancel anytime through your Apple App Store or Google Play Store subscription settings. Your Pro features will remain active until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account reverts to the Free tier — no data is lost.

How do I delete my account and data? ▼

You can delete your account from the Profile settings in the app, or by emailing privacy@enerzee.app. We will delete your personal data within 30 days of the request.

Is my health data safe? ▼

Your data is stored on Supabase with row-level security, meaning each user can only access their own data. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not sell your personal data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
