Responsible AI at EnerZee

How we build a coach you can trust with your health.

EnerZee's coach gives evidence-based nutrition and training guidance. Because it talks to people about their health, we hold it to a higher bar than a typical chatbot. Here's how we keep it safe, accurate, and honest — and where its limits are.

01

What the coach will not do

EnerZee coaches wellness. It is built to know the edge of its lane and stop there.

  • It does not diagnose conditions, prescribe treatment, or change your medication.
  • For medical, medication, pregnancy, minor (under-18), mental-health, or emergency situations, it stops coaching and routes you to a qualified professional.
  • It refuses dangerous requests — extreme calorie deficits, performance-enhancing drugs (PEDs), diuretics, dehydration tactics, starvation cuts, peak-week manipulation, or "pushing through" warning symptoms.
  • It flags red-flag symptoms — for example, chest tightness during exercise — as reasons to seek care, not to keep training.
02

We test it — and publish the score

We benchmark the real coach against a standalone general-purpose model on expert-graded health-safety and nutrition cases, and re-run that benchmark on every release so quality can't quietly slip.

We publish the score and the date. See the head-to-head results on our Trust & Evidence section.

03

Evidence first

Guidance is grounded in clinical guidelines and peer-reviewed research — with specific numbers and the reasoning behind them, not vague encouragement. When the coach makes a claim, it can show you the studies behind it.

05

Your data

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell it. EnerZee is built to comply with India's DPDP Act and the EU's GDPR, including your rights to access and delete your information.

Pending confirmationWhether your health data is used to train AI models — to be stated plainly here once confirmed. (We will not leave this ambiguous on the live site.)
06

Honest limitations

EnerZee is a wellness coach, not a medical provider. AI can be wrong. Please verify important decisions with a qualified professional — and tell us when the coach gets something wrong, so we can fix it.

07

When something goes wrong

Every coach reply has a thumbs-up / thumbs-down control. Flagged answers are reviewed by our team and feed back into the benchmark we test against — so a single bad answer becomes a permanent test case the coach has to pass on every future release.

EnerZee is an evidence-based wellness coach, not a licensed medical provider. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace your doctor, dietitian, or emergency services. For medical, medication, pregnancy, or urgent concerns, consult a qualified professional.
