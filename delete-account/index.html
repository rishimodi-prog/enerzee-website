Delete Account

Account and data deletion requests

You can request deletion of your EnerZee account and associated personal data from inside the app or from this page.

Delete From The App

  1. Open EnerZee and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to Profile, then Account.
  3. Choose Delete Account and confirm the deletion prompts.

Request Deletion By Email

If you cannot access the app, email us from the address linked to your EnerZee account. Include "Delete my EnerZee account" in the subject line.

Email privacy@enerzee.app

What We Delete

When your request is verified, we delete your account and associated personal data, including profile data, food logs, workout logs, progress history, connected-device records, AI Coach memory, and chat history.

What May Be Retained

We may retain limited records where required for legal, tax, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or regulatory compliance. We may also retain anonymized or aggregated data that cannot identify you.

Timing

We aim to complete verified deletion requests within 30 days. If we need more information to verify ownership of the account, we will contact you at the email address used for the request.

Subscriptions

Deleting your EnerZee account does not automatically cancel an active Apple App Store or Google Play subscription. Please cancel your subscription in your Apple or Google account settings to stop future billing.

Questions

For privacy questions or data requests, contact privacy@enerzee.app.
